United States Secret Service in the Media


Scandal USSS Movies Success


The media seems to show the Secret Service agents as protecting, fighting heroes. Movies that recently came out involving the Secret Service are: White House Down and Olympus Has Fallen. These action packed movies show that these heroes do everything to protect the President and portrays them as honorable people who want to protect their families as well.

Hollywood celebrates the Secret Service as powerful human heroes, while the news media has an odd balance of making them out as great heroes or unprofessional men. Recently though the Secret Service is getting a very negative view from the backlash of Colombia, El Salvador, and Amsterdam. The Secret Service can keep themselves out of the media spot light unlike its fellow departments that land in more hot water. Movies see them as heroes while news currently thinks there can be adjustments to the agency, especially against any form of racism.
