United States Secret Service's Popular Movies


  • White House Down- A Man, who hopes to become a Secret Service agent, gets caught up in a takeover of the White House and has to protect the President and his daughter.
  • Olympus Has Fallen-Dishonored ex-Secret Service agent gets swept up in a terrorist attack and has to save the President from kidnappers.
  • Brake-An agent is kidnapped and has to live through mental and physical torture without giving any information about the President.
  • The Sentinel- A Secret Service agent is wrongly accused of trying to assassinate the President, but has to do everything to prove their innocence and save the President.
  • In Her Line of Fire-A female agent assigned to the vice President has to save him from kidnappers thanks to their plane going down in the Pacific.
  • The Interpreter-A female Secret Service agent is assigned to investigate an interpreter that overheard an assassination plot.
  • Spartan-An agent investigates the kidnapping of a high-ranking government official’s daughter.
  • Double Identity-A doctor’s identity is stolen and a Secret Service agent must help her stay alive.
